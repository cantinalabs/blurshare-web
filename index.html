Why BlurShare

Your screen holds more than the window you meant to share.

Private DMs, client emails, half-written tabs, personal notifications — one wrong share can cost a client, a role, or your privacy. BlurShare removes the risk.

01 / PRIVACY

Keep private things private

Conversations, journals, and personal tabs stay invisible to your audience, not just minimized.

02 / LEAKS

Prevent data leaks

Block mail clients, specific browsers, or any app that should never flash across a client call.

03 / DOXX

Stop self-doxxing

Control exactly what identifying details observers can see on your personal computer.

04 / FOCUS

Look professional

Features

A surgical privacy toolkit for your screen.

Precision controls that stay out of your way until the moment you need them.

Allowlist & Blocklist

Define exactly which apps stay visible and which get blurred. Works per-app or per-window title.

Multiple Profiles

Switch between Work, Streaming, Client Calls, and Personal configurations in a single click.

Panic Mode

One hotkey to blur every window on every screen. Your emergency brake.

Custom Hotkeys

Bind ⌘⇧B to toggle the shield. Your keyboard, your rules.

Click-Through Blur

Drag and rearrange windows underneath the blur without ever disabling it.

Auto-Enable on Share

BlurShare flips on the moment Zoom, Slack, Teams, Discord, or OBS start sharing your screen — so you never have to remember.

Notification Blur Zone

Place a resizable blurred region wherever your notifications appear. Full coverage, user resizable.

Triggers that just work

Auto-enable when your browser requests camera or microphone access. Auto-enable on native macOS screenshots and screen recordings. BlurShare anticipates the moment you need it — so you don't have to.

How it works

Three steps. Then forget it exists.

BlurShare is built to fade into your workflow. Set it once, trust it forever.

Pick a profile

Start with a preset for Work, Streaming, or Client Calls. Or build your own from scratch.

Set your rules

Drag apps into your allowlist or blocklist. Resize your notification blur zone. Bind your hotkeys.

Share with confidence

BlurShare wakes up the moment you start sharing and stays out of your way the rest of the time.
